Our Mission


Millions of people across the world lack basic health care and/or can't even afford it. Furthermore, patients ignore their symptoms assuming that it is normal, but later it turns to be critical disease which can be in advanced stage. We propose an AI solution that could be a helper to and potential substitue for medical workers, so patients can know probable diagnosis that they are suffering from.

Your personal wellness visit with Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing

Diganosis


With the use of natural language processing, our artifically intellegent doctor breaks down what you say and develops a diagnosis depending on your needs. 

Acessibility


Most people don't have a Doctor to talk to regularly about their health issues, but what most people do have is access to the internet. With just a camera and a mic, people can receive a wellness visit. 

Artificial Intelligence


Our "VDoc" makes use of a variety of brand new artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to receive information on the user through a screen and give an appropriate assessement of their health.
